Really nice to have a Vietnamese place in Abingdon! We had the iced lemon jasmine tea, papaya salad with tofu, and veggie vermicelli (which came with little veggie spring rolls and deep fried tofu), and everything was super. Portions very generous - couldn't finish the noodles. Lovely decor inside as well, very comfortable and welcoming. Much needed addition to Abingdon's cafe scene! Will be back to try their other dishes - what's also nice is that there is plenty here to keep veggies/vegans happy.
